Our Mission


ONTechOpportunities is a website dedicated to providing High School Students in Ontario insignt into Computer Science opportuntities and extracurriculars. Most student express regret in not knowing about opportunities until it is too late or being unprepared to tackle applying to programs. Increasingly, there has been a heavier emphasis on extracurriculars as they provide an environement for students to hone their professional skills and help them actually determine their true interests in a field. Feel free to scrool through our extensive dataset of online courses, competitions, internships, non-profits, clubs, project ideas, and summer programs and see which ones spark your interest!
